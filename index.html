Jian Yang

Contact Me
I obtained my B.S. degree and Ph.D. at Beihang University, as a joint Phd student between Beihang University and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), supervised by Prof. Zhoujun Li and Dr. Ming Zhou. I am currently a Research Scientist at Alibaba Qwen Team, focusing on Large Language Models (LLMs), including code, pos-training, and reasoning and serving as reviewer or AC for ACL, EMNLP NAACL, and ICLR.
